Abantus Saga

Start your own saga. Abantus Saga 2 takes place in a space-like atmosphere, and your task is to line up like-colored squares to form rows or columns. Designed for iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch, and requires iOS 8 or later.
Download for Apple iOS

Keep it simple

Abantus Saga 2 is an innovative puzzle game setting place in a dark atmospheric space universe.

Thanks for the randomly generated exciting and challenging levels awaiting for you, you might have the best gaming experiement you’ve ever had. What is more! You can challenge your friends or a random stranger in the multiplayer mode.

  • Intuitive one-touch controls combined with innovative dynamic level design
  • More than 370 truly unique levels
  • Multiplayer mode
  • Challenge Mode
  • iCloud support

Easy to learn, hard to master

All you have to do is to organize equivalent coloured squares to form a row or column, and watch out, the time is running! Boost your score by making awesome rewarding combos then share your scores and compete with your friends via Gamecenter and Facebook.

Challenge yourself, and get to the top of the ladder while completing achievments. Compare your skills and compete with friends or random players in multiplayer mode. Play along all the 370 challenging levels in single player mode. With the help of iCloud support you can continue playing on each of your devices.
